About Us | Who We Are

Founded in 2024 by a Nigerian Man | Psalmson

Accel Speed has grown into a global leader in the automotive industry. With a rich heritage of engineering expertise and a relentless pursuit of innovation, we have created some of the most iconic vehicles on the road today. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every car we produce meets the highest standards of quality, performance, and safety.

Our Vision

To create good vehicles

Our vision is to redefine mobility by creating vehicles that are not only powerful and efficient but also sustainable and accessible. We believe in a future where transportation is seamless, connected, and environmentally friendly, and we are committed to leading the way in this transformation.

Our Mission

To create good vehicles

To provide our customers with the most advanced, reliable, and stylish vehicles on the market, while upholding our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and exceptional customer service.
